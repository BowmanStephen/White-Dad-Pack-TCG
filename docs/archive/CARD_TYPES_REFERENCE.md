### Special Card Types (Mechanics & Events)

#### EVENT (SHITSHOW SCENARIOS)
**Effect**: One-time use cards that trigger immediate game effects
**Duration**: Varies (instant to 3+ turns)
**Examples**: 
  - Spice Melange Masturbation Madness (Dune crossover)
  - Coca-Cola Cum-Catastrophe (brand crossover)
  - Holiday Fireworks Fuckfest (seasonal)
**Current Count**: 1 card
**Planned Count**: 15 cards total

**Mechanics**:
- Boost specific card types for limited turns
- Trigger damage on all opponents
- Change battle conditions globally
- Unlock special attack sequences

#### TERRAIN (SUBURBAN SHITFIELDS)
**Effect**: Battlefield modifiers that persist across multiple turns
**Duration**: Stays in play until replaced
**Examples**:
  - Arrakis Arse-Arena (Dune crossover)
  - Soda Fountain Fuck-Field (Coca-Cola)
  - My Backyard Fuck-Zone (standard terrain)
**Current Count**: 1 card
**Planned Count**: 10 cards total

**Mechanics**:
- Boost specific card types permanently
- Apply stat penalties to opponents
- Create home field advantage
- Enable special abilities only on this terrain

#### EVOLUTION (MIDLIFE CRISIS MUTATIONS)
**Effect**: Upgrade cards that boost another DICKTATOR DAD's stats permanently
**Target**: Any DICKTATOR DAD (requires attachment)
**Examples**:
  - Dad to Dilf Transformation
  - Sports Car Syndrome
  - Hair Transplant Hero
**Current Count**: 1 card
**Planned Count**: 8 cards total

**Mechanics**:
- Apply permanent stat boost (+20 across board or +30 to one stat)
- Unlock new abilities on attached card
- One evolution per dad (or stack rules TBD)
- Can be replaced by newer evolution

#### CURSE (DAD DAMNATIONS)
**Effect**: Negative status effect cards targeting opponent dads
**Duration**: 3+ turns until removed
**Examples**:
  - The Thermostat Touch (wife controls temp)
  - The Bad Back (reduced mobility)
  - Credit Card Debt (financial ruin)
**Current Count**: 1 card
**Planned Count**: 10 cards total

**Mechanics**:
- Apply -X stat penalties (typically -20 to -30)
- Restrict ability usage
- Deal ongoing damage per turn
- Require removal card or turn expiration

#### TRAP (SUBURBAN SUCKERPUNCHES)
**Effect**: Face-down triggered cards that activate when opponent plays specific card type
**Trigger**: When opponent plays a card matching trap criteria
**Examples**:
  - HOA Ambush (triggers on lawn/backyard cards)
  - Wife's Surprise (triggers on late-night cards)
  - Credit Card Statement (triggers on purchase cards)
**Current Count**: 1 card
**Planned Count**: 8 cards total

**Mechanics**:
- Deal X damage when trigger condition met
- Apply status effect to triggering card
- Return card to hand instead of playing
- Can be triggered once or multiple times

#### ITEM (EQUIPMENT & ACCESSORIES)
**Effect**: Equipment cards that boost attached DICKTATOR DAD's stats
**Target**: Any DICKTATOR DAD (must be equipped)
**Examples**:
  - Weber Wiener 3000 Grill (+30 grillSkill)
  - La-Z-Boy Lust Throne (+40 napPower)
  - Craftsman Cock-Wrench Box (+30 fixIt)
**Current Count**: 4 cards
**Planned Count**: 30 cards total

**Mechanics**:
- Permanent stat boost while equipped
- Can equip multiple items (up to 3-5 per dad)
- Discard to replace with other item
- Some items unlock abilities (e.g., grill cards)
- Rarity affects power level and equip limits
